Information about Crop Residue

Every year, during the winters the air quality dramatically deteriorates in Northern India. The major cause of this is stubble or crop residue burning by farmers in Punjab and Haryana. To provide a sustainable alternative to our farmers our technology gives them a workable substitute to burning crop residue for the purpose of utilising crop waste and addressing the issue of rising air pollution, as well as a high-quality biomass supply for power plants. Although burning crop residue is the cheapest method available to them for getting rid of crop waste, it is not environmentally friendly. Because of this, all that is required for our system to determine how much residue a crop produces is a picture of the crop. After which we tell them the exact percentage of residue that particular crop generates

Information about us
